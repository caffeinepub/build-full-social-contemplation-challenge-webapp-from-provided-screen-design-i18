# Deployment Verification Checklist

This document provides a step-by-step guide to verify that a deployment has completed successfully and the live app is functioning correctly.

## Post-Deployment Verification Steps

### 1. Confirm Deployment Completed
- ✅ Check that the deployment process finished without errors
- ✅ Verify the deployment logs show successful completion
- ✅ Confirm the canister IDs are correct and accessible

### 2. Verify Live App Loads
- ✅ Open the live app URL in a browser
- ✅ Confirm the app loads without a persistent white screen or spinner
- ✅ Check browser console for any critical errors (ignore warnings)
